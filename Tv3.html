I have to wonder about the quality of the circuit board used by LG.
It is possible that the issue occurred due to a faulty board and not
a voltage surge. It is unfortunate LG doesn't cover such
issues even during the warranty period even though it's only
a 2 week old product. It has been a disappointing experience
for me with LG and would not ever be buying or recommending an LG product again.
